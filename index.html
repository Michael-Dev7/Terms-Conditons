Wedding Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payment

  • A non-refundable down payment of ₱1,000 is required to secure your booking.
  • The remaining balance must be settled immediately after the wedding celebration.
  • Failure to make the down payment may result in automatic cancellation of the booking.

2. Additional Fees

  • Transportation Fees: Additional charges may apply depending on the event location.
  • Extra Coverage: The wedding package includes 8 hours of coverage (covering preparation, ceremony, and reception). If the event exceeds this time, an additional fee of ₱500 per hour will be charged for extended photography services.

3. Coverage Time Limits

  • Wedding Event: Coverage is limited to 8 hours, including preparation, ceremony, and reception. Any additional time beyond this will be charged at ₱500 per hour.
  • Prenuptial Session: Coverage is limited to 4 hours (excluding travel time between locations).
  • Additional Time Requests: Extra hours must be requested in advance and will be charged accordingly.

4. Delivery of Photos

  • Soft Copies: Delivered within 2-7 working days after the event via Google Drive.
  • Hard Copies: Delivered within 2-5 days after the client has selected photos from the soft copies.

5. Cancellation & Rescheduling

  • The down payment is non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Rescheduling is allowed subject to availability. Clients must notify at least 1 week before the scheduled event.
  • In case of unforeseen circumstances (e.g., extreme weather, emergencies), both parties may discuss alternative arrangements.
